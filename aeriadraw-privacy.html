AeriaDraw Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 16, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how AeriaDraw handles user data. AeriaDraw is a spatial drawing app built for Apple Vision Pro. It is developed by a small student team as an independent project.

Information We Collect

AeriaDraw does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or contact details.
  • We do not collect analytics, usage statistics, or device identifiers.
  • We do not track users across apps or websites.

Drawings and Local Data

All drawings and related project files created in AeriaDraw are stored locally on your device only. They are not uploaded to any server by us, and we do not have access to your drawings.

Third-Party Services

AeriaDraw does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking libraries. The app does not communicate with external services except those required by the Apple operating system and App Store infrastructure.
