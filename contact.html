About

ADAM Yuzhen Zhang is a fifth-year undergraduate student at Cornell University. He majors in Architecture and minors in Computer Science. His interests lie in the interdisciplinary study of VR/AR technology and architecture. He is investigating technology's role in architectural history, theory, urbanism, and design, to innovate design workflows that could help architects adapt for the future.
